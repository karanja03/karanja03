## 👩‍💻 About Me

I'm **Wambui** — a full-stack and mobile software engineer with 3+ years of experience building production systems that real people rely on. I've shipped everything from a custom ERP for a hospitality business to a digital newspaper platform serving 20,000+ daily readers across East Africa. I care deeply about the whole product — not just the code, but the design decisions, the architecture, and the experience on the other end.

Right now I'm in **Tallinn, Estonia** through the EU-funded **Digital Explorers Programme**, working as a Full-Stack Developer at **Kood/Jõhvi** and studying **Interaction Design & HCI** at Tallinn University — which means I think about usability just as much as I think about system design.

- 🌍 **Nairobi → Tallinn** — shaped by two very different tech ecosystems
- 🌱 **Lifelong learner** — every new codebase is a chance to think differently
- ⚡ **20 → 20,000+ users** — I've worked both ends of the scale curve
- 🏗️ **End-to-end ownership** — architecture, APIs, databases, deployment, UX
- 🤝 **She Code Africa** — passionate about women in tech and community mentorship
